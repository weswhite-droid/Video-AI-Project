📊 Key Pilot Context SF Tower · Nov–Jan ▼
Important context: The original Cobalt AI pilot at SF Tower (launched Sept 2025) was primarily focused on Cobalt + Service Cloud integration — it later expanded to include Video Analytics/Video AI elements. No formal division of responsibilities was documented between the ProServe/REWS team and the GOC before go-live, which likely explains why GOC never formally agreed to own tailgate alarm reviews. This must be corrected before any Phase 2 site activation.
