🚀 Slack Sync Setup Wizard

Click the buttons below to open each required page. Follow the instructions step-by-step.

📱 Step 1: Create Slack App

What to do:

  1. Click "Create New App"
  2. Select "From scratch"
  3. App Name: Video AI Dashboard Sync
  4. Pick your Salesforce workspace
  5. Click "Create App"

🔐 Step 2: Add Permissions

After creating the app, you'll be on the app settings page.

What to do:

  1. Click "OAuth & Permissions" in left sidebar
  2. Scroll to "Scopes" → "Bot Token Scopes"
  3. Click "Add an OAuth Scope" and add each of these:
    • channels:history
    • channels:read
    • files:read
    • groups:history
    • groups:read

💾 Step 3: Install App & Copy Token

What to do:

  1. Scroll to top of "OAuth & Permissions" page
  2. Click "Install to Workspace"
  3. Review permissions and click "Allow"
  4. COPY THE BOT USER OAUTH TOKEN (starts with xoxb-)
  5. Save it somewhere - you'll need it in Step 5!

💬 Step 4: Invite Bot to Channel

What to do:

  1. Go to channel #proj-gss-video-ai-working-group
  2. Type: /invite @Video AI Dashboard Sync
  3. Press Enter
  4. Bot will appear in channel members

🔑 Step 5: Add Token to GitHub

What to do:

  1. Click "New repository secret"
  2. Name: SLACK_BOT_TOKEN
  3. Value: Paste your token from Step 3
  4. Click "Add secret"

🧪 Step 6: Test the Sync

What to do:

  1. Click "Run workflow" dropdown button
  2. Click green "Run workflow" button
  3. Wait ~1 minute for it to complete
  4. Look for green checkmark ✅

✅ All Done!

Once you complete all steps, your dashboard will automatically sync from Slack every 30 minutes!
